Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before your escape adventure. Can't find your answer? Give us a call!

ARE CHILDREN ALLOWED?

Of course, we pride ourselves on being family friendly! Our only policy is that if a majority of the players are 13 years of age or younger, then we do require a paying adult, 18 years of age or older to be in the room.

WHAT IS THE COST OF A TICKET?

Easy question! Our tickets start at only $35.00 per person, but the more players you have, the cheaper it gets. Oh, and don't forget, kids 8 and younger are always free!

ARE WALK-INS ALLOWED?

Absolutely they are! The only thing we want to share with you is the following:

  • If all the rooms are booked up, you may have to wait.
  • Reservations do take priority over walk-ins.
  • Staff limitations may prevent us from taking walk-ins.

WILL WE BE IN A ROOM WITH STRANGERS?

Only if you want to! When you make the booking online, there is an option to 'Make Private' — it's that simple. If you don't select that option, then you can have the fun of playing with new friends!

DO YOU HAVE A CANCELLATION POLICY?

We sure do. You may cancel up to 24 hours prior to your booking. If this makes you nervous, have no fear — we offer booking insurance!

HOW DOES THE BOOKING INSURANCE WORK?

Great question! When you make your reservation, simply add one (1) booking insurance to your booking. With this, you may cancel or reschedule at anytime prior to your booking and get a full refund minus the cost of the booking insurance.

IS YOUR LOCATION ADA FRIENDLY?

Yes, we are! All rooms can accommodate ADA and wheelchair access.

CAN WE TAKE PHOTOS IN THE ROOM?

So sorry, but photos are not allowed in the room. I know it sounds silly, but this helps to keep the fun secrets of the game just that... secret! But have no fear, we will do a photo of your group when your game is over.

STILL HAVE QUESTIONS?

We're here to help! Give us a call or send us a message.
